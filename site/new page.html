Our Core Expertise

Brinda Industries delivers multiple range of pressure gauges the summary as mentioned below

BPG – All SS Pressure Gauges Bourdon Operated

Boudon operated pressure gauges used in following mentioned application as Chemical & Petrochemical, pneumatic & hydraulic, power generation & distribution, refrigeration & water treatment plant, engine & compressor, liquid & gaseous media that will not obstruct the pressure system

Features:

  • All SS case & measuring system
  • Rugged construction
  • Dry or liquid filled version
  • Choice of wetted parts
  • Socket-case, direct welded
  • Standard Follow EN 837-1 / IS 3624
BTG – Industrial Thermometer Bimetal Type

Such type of gauges are used in refinery & oil exploration, fertilizer industry, power generation, power distribution, pharmaceutical & cement industry, sugar industries, food and beverages

Features:

  • SS case & stem
  • Stem length available from 50mm
  • Bottom / Back / Every angle mounting
  • Silicon oil filled (Optional)
  • With or without thermowell
  • External zero adjustment
  • Standard followed EN 13190
IPG – All SS Pressure Gauges Industrial Bourdon Operated

This type of gauges are used in several industries pharmaceutical, fertilizer, engine & compressor, sip building industries, refrigerant & water treatment plant, on shore & offshore, liquid & gaseous media that will not obstruct the pressure system.

Features:

  • All SS cases & measuring system
  • Socket-case, direct welded
  • Rugged construction
  • Choice of wetted parts
  • Dry or liquid filled version
  • Standard followed EN 837-1 / IS-3624
GTG – Industrial Thermometer Filled System

The application of GTG – Industrial Thermometer filled system is in corrosive media / environments, food & beverages, pharmaceutical industry, pulp & paper industry, refinery & oil exploration, cement industry and many more

Features:

  • Inert gas filled expansion system
  • Stainless steel case & stem
  • With or without thermowell
  • Glycerin / silicon oil filled
  • External Zero / Electrical Contacts (Optional)
  • Standard Followed EN 13190
EPG – All SS Pressure Gauges Electric Contact Bourdon Operated

This type of pressure gauges are used under to control the electric operation of compressor, pumps, motors, presses, hydraulics and pneumatic equipment. Furthermore they are used in several industries like chemical & petrochemical, refineries & oil exploration, on shore & off shore, ship building and last but not least power generation and distribution

Features:

  • All SS Cases & Measuring system
  • Socket-case, direct welded
  • Rugged construction
  • Choice of wetted parts
  • Dry or liquid filled version
  • Standard follow EN 837-1 / IS 3624
TPG – Test Gauge (Acc ± 0.25%) Bourdon operated

The TPG – test gauge bourdon operated used in calibration / testing in laboratory and it fits for various process industries and all gaseous / liquid media that will not construct the pressure system.

Features:

  • Stainless steel case & high pressure measuring system
  • High resolution dial
  • Anti parallax mirror band (eye friendly)
  • Socket-case, direct welded
  • Calibration certificate with traceability to national / international standards
  • Standard followed EN837-1
